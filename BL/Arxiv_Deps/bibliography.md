## Quoted results from other places

1. **Machado--Ramos stability paper.**

   Reference: J. M. Machado and J. P. G. Ramos, *Quantitative stability for
   Brascamp--Lieb and moment measures*, arXiv:2511.22636v2.

   Used in the current paper only for the contextual abstract phrase about
   sharp uniform `L^1` stability. The proof of the `L^2` counterexample does not
   depend on this result.

2. **Brascamp--Lieb inequality.**

   Reference: H. J. Brascamp and E. H. Lieb, *On extensions of the
   Brunn--Minkowski and Prékopa--Leindler theorems, including inequalities for
   log-concave functions, and with an application to the diffusion equation*,
   J. Funct. Anal. 22 (1976), 366--389.

   Precise statement needed if formalizing the quoted theorem: for smooth
   strictly convex `phi : R^d -> R` with
   `0 < ∫ exp(-phi) dx < infinity`, and suitable `f`,
   `Var_{rho_phi}(f) <= ∫ <(D^2 phi)^{-1} grad f, grad f> d rho_phi`, with
   equality only for `f(x)=a·grad phi(x)+b` among `L^2(rho_phi)` functions.
